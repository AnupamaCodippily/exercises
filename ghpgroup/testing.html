Dyna-Glo 41,000 BTU Deluxe Hammered Bronze Patio Heater

$219.99

Turn your cold and dull nights into warm and cozy evenings underneath the propane powered (20 lb propane tank not included) Dyna-Glo Deluxe Patio Heater. Even when the summer has come and gone, this powerful 41,000 BTU heater will help keep you warm with its variable heat setting. While you are toasting wine with friends, you'll find that the beautiful hammered bronze heater will accent your patio. The piezo ignition makes it a breeze to start, and a weighted base keeps it steady and secure. When the seasons change and it's hot outside, the unit has wheels: just roll the heater into storage. Don't let another chilly night keep you inside. Get out of the house and into the fun with the Dyna-Glo Round Patio Heater.
